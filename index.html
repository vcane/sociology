Event Description

We are looking for women 20 and older who are/had suffered from miscarriages, abortion, waiting to have a baby, finances, and those with careers or starting to have their career.

Many individuals with these reasons may develop some uncomfortableness while talking about your reasons, which can lead to guilt. This research does not seek to hurt, harm, and make an individual feel unsafe or unwelcome; but simply to research birth rates declining in Topeka, Kansas. All those who wish to do this research, may contact us.
